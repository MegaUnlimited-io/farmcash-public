    What is FarmCash?

    FarmCash is a mobile rewards app that turns waiting time into fun. Instead of the typical boring "complete offers, get paid eventually" model, we've built a farming game around the payout delay.

    Here's how it works:

    1. Complete offers (games, surveys, trials) → Earn seeds
    2. Plant crops → Choose growth time (4 hours to 30 days)
    3. Water daily → Speed up growth (optional engagement)
    4. Harvest → Seeds convert to real cash
    5. Cash out → Withdraw via PayPal

    The hook: Patient players earn more. A 30-day crop yields 130% vs 75% for a 4-hour crop. We turn the payout delay that frustrates users into the game itself.

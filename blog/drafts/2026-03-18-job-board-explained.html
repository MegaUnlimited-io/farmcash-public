    📱 What Is the Job Board?

    If you've ever used a rewards app like FreeCash, Mistplay, or Swagbucks, you've seen what the industry calls an "offerwall" before. We call it something different: the Job Board.

    In simple terms: The Job Board is your daily list of games, apps, and tasks that reward you for completing specific actions. Think of it as the town bulletin board where folks post jobs that pay in seeds.

    Example Jobs

    • "Reach Level 10 in [Game Name] → Earn 250 seeds"
    • "Download [App] and complete tutorial → Earn 100 seeds"
    • "Complete this 5-minute survey → Earn 75 seeds"

    The catch with most rewards apps: They're boring. Click offer → Complete task → Get paid → Repeat. It's transactional, not engaging.

    That's not FarmCash.

    🌱 FarmCash's Twist: Jobs Earn Seeds, Seeds Grow Crops

    We asked ourselves: "What if earning rewards felt like playing a game, not doing chores?"

    That's where FarmCash is different.

    Here's the full loop:

    1. Check the Job Board (games, apps, surveys)
    2. Take on a job (reach a level, complete tutorial, etc.)
    3. Earn SEEDS (our in-game currency)
    4. Plant seeds in your virtual farm
    5. Grow crops (patience = higher yield)
    6. Harvest crops → Convert to cash
    7. Cash out via PayPal

    The key difference: Seeds aren't cash yet. They're potential. You plant them, nurture them, and watch them grow. The longer you wait, the more they're worth.

    🪙 How Do You Earn Seeds?

    The Job Board offers three main types of jobs:

    1. Game Jobs (Primary)

    Most of the best jobs in FarmCash are mobile games. Here's how it works:

    • Browse available game jobs on the Job Board
    • Download + install the game (free)
    • Complete specific challenges:
      • "Reach Level 10"
      • "Complete the tutorial"
      • "Defeat the first boss"
      • "Build your first [building]"
    • Higher difficulty = more seeds

    Job Types: Quick, Task, and Signup

    You'll see different job types on the board:

    • Quick Jobs: Install an app, done in 5 minutes
    • Task Jobs: Multi-step challenges (reach level X, complete Y tasks)
    • Signup Jobs: Register for a service or trial

    Task jobs typically pay the most seeds because they require more effort.

    💡 Pro Tip: Optional Purchases

    Some game jobs allow optional purchases for the highest rewards. These typically reward MORE seeds (because the advertiser pays us more). But spending is NEVER required to progress in FarmCash — you can complete all jobs for free if you're patient.

    2. App Jobs

    Similar to game jobs, but for non-gaming apps:

    • Download the app (free)
    • Complete onboarding or specific actions
    • Earn seeds

    Examples: Finance apps, shopping apps, productivity tools, etc.

    3. Survey Jobs

    Short surveys market research companies pay for:

    • 3-10 minute surveys
    • Answer honestly (they have quality checks)
    • Earn seeds upon completion

    Note: Survey availability varies by region and demographics. Not everyone will see survey jobs all the time.

    🌾 Why Seeds Instead of Direct Cash?

    This is the question everyone asks. Here's why:

    1. The Patience Mechanic

    In most rewards apps, you earn $0.50 → wait until you hit $20 → cash out. It's a grind.

    In FarmCash, seeds GROW. A seed planted today could be worth 75% of its potential tomorrow... or 130% if you wait a month.

    Patience is rewarded. This isn't just philosophy — it's baked into the math.

    2. The Game Layer

    Seeds let us add actual game mechanics:

    • Crop tiers: Better crops unlock as you level up
    • Plot expansion: More land = more crops = more earnings
    • Risk/reward: Short-term crops (safe, low yield) vs. long-term crops (risky, high yield)
    • Strategy: Do you harvest early for quick cash? Or wait for maximum yield?

    This is what makes FarmCash unique: It's not just a rewards app. It's a farming sim WITH rewards.

    3. Psychological Benefits

    Direct cash creates a "grind" mentality. Seeds create a "growth" mentality.

    Mindset Shift

    • Cash: "I need $20. How fast can I grind offers?"
    • Seeds: "I planted tomato crops. They'll be ready in 24 hours. I'll check back tomorrow."

    One feels like work. The other feels like a game.

    💰 So... How Much Can You Actually Earn?

    Honest answer: It depends.

    Factors:

    • How many jobs you complete
    • Which jobs you choose (task jobs = more seeds than quick jobs)
    • How patient you are with crops (longer = higher yield)
    • Whether you make optional purchases (faster progress, but costs money)

    Realistic Expectations

    • Casual player (1-2 jobs/week, patient crops): $10-30/month
    • Active player (5-10 jobs/week, optimized): $30-100/month
    • Hardcore player (daily jobs, strategic purchases): $100-300+/month

    Important: This is supplemental income, not a replacement for a job. Think "beer money" or "coffee money," not "rent money."

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our Services. The categories of data we collect include:

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account with FarmCash, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Username or display name
  • Password (stored in encrypted form)
  • Country of residence
  • Date of birth or age confirmation

2.2 Identity Verification Data (KYC)

To prevent fraud and comply with applicable laws, we may require identity verification before processing withdrawals. This verification is conducted through third-party identity verification partners and may include:

  • Government-issued identification documents (passport, driver's license, national ID card)
  • Selfie photographs or video for liveness verification
  • Information extracted from identification documents (name, date of birth, document number, issuing country, expiry date)
  • Biometric data derived from facial images for identity matching purposes
  • Verification outcomes and risk assessment signals

Important: Our identity verification partners may process biometric data (such as facial geometry) to verify your identity. This processing is necessary for fraud prevention and platform security. Where required by law, we will obtain your explicit consent before such processing.

2.3 Device and Technical Data

We automatically collect certain technical information when you use our Services:

  • Device identifiers (including advertising identifiers such as Google Advertising ID)
  • Device type, model, and manufacturer
  • Operating system and version
  • IP address
  • Approximate geographic location (derived from IP address)
  • Time zone settings
  • Language preferences
  • Network connection type

2.4 Usage Data

We collect information about how you interact with our Services:

  • App usage patterns and session duration
  • Offers viewed, started, and completed
  • Virtual currency (Seeds) earned and redeemed
  • Withdrawal history and preferences
  • Referral program participation
  • Survey responses and participation data
  • Customer support interactions

2.5 Offer Engagement Data

When you engage with offers provided through our advertising partners, we receive and process:

  • Offer completion status and timestamps
  • App installation verification data
  • In-app event tracking (for verifying offer completion requirements)
  • Attribution and conversion data
  • Survey response data (where applicable)

2.6 Payment Information

When you request a withdrawal, we collect information necessary to process your payment:

  • PayPal email address (for PayPal withdrawals)
  • Gift card delivery email address
  • Transaction history and payment preferences

Note: We do not directly collect or store bank account details or credit/debit card numbers. All payment processing is handled by third-party payment providers.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including to satisfy legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

Our standard retention periods are as follows:

  • Account Data: Retained for as long as your account remains active, plus an additional period following account closure for legal and fraud prevention purposes.
  • Identity Verification Data: We retain verification results, timestamps, and risk signals as long as needed to provide services and for fraud prevention (typically 3 years). Raw images (documents/selfies) are generally retained by our verification partners only as long as strictly necessary for verification, audit, and compliance purposes.
  • Transaction Data: Retained for at least 7 years to comply with tax and accounting requirements.
  • Usage Data: Generally retained for up to 3 years for analytics purposes, unless anonymized earlier.
  • Fraud Prevention Data: May be retained indefinitely for platform security and to prevent repeat offenders from creating new accounts.

Inactive Accounts: If you do not use our Services for an extended period (as specified in our Terms of Service), your account may be deemed inactive. Inactive accounts may be terminated, and any unredeemed virtual currency may expire in accordance with our Terms.
